Women put a lot of time and effort into how we present ourselves. Whether it’s to maintain personal standards, professional mentality, social appearance, or simply just to be a female, there are many frustrations and expectations we put upon ourselves to achieve a level of “put-together-ness”.

As a woman, I know the frustrations and indecisiveness of choosing the perfect outfit, shoes, lipstick, handbag, and more specifically, a nail color to act as the final accessory. As someone who frequents nail salons once a month, I knew my same frustrations were similar to the girl’s standing next to me in front of the ‘giant wall of color’, unsure which color would complete our “look.”

In order to get to the root of my observed problem, I chose to create a much leaner problem statement and conduct research to support my hypothesis that women have a hard time selecting a nail color from a large assortment of colors.
